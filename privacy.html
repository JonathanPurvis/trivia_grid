Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here is exactly what we collect, why, and how.

Effective Date: January 1, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to TriviaGrid ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our website located at TriviaGrid (the "Site") and the game tools available on it (the "Service").

By using the Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our Site.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect, particularly regarding student data:

  • We do not collect student names, ages, grades, or any personally identifiable student information.
  • We do not require accounts, logins, or registration of any kind.
  • We do not transmit your custom questions, team names, game scores, or daily streak data to our servers. All of this information is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage on your own device.
  • We do not sell personal information to third parties.
  • We are not a data broker and do not participate in data brokerage.

3. Information We Do Collect

Like virtually all websites, we collect certain non-personally-identifiable information automatically when you visit our Site:

Log Data: Our web server may automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website. This may include your browser type and version, the pages of our Site that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other standard statistics. This information is used solely for maintaining and improving the Site and is not linked to any personal identity.

Analytics: We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to help us understand how our Site is used. These services may collect information about your use of the Site and report website trends without identifying individual visitors. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies: Our Site may use "cookies" — small data files stored in your browser — to enable basic site functionality and to help analytics services work. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some parts of the Site may not function properly without cookies.

4. Advertising

TriviaGrid displays advertisements served by third-party ad networks, which may include Google AdSense. These ad networks may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our Site and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting aboutads.info.

We do not control the data collection practices of third-party ad networks. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party ad networks that may be serving ads on this Site.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

TriviaGrid is intended for use by teachers and other adult educators. The Site is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

The game tool itself is designed for classroom use and may be used by children under teacher supervision. However, the game does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information from students — all game data remains in the student's own browser.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the address below and we will take steps to remove that information from our systems.

6. FERPA

TriviaGrid does not collect, store, or process any student educational records as defined under the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA). Teachers use this Site to facilitate classroom activities, but no student data is transmitted to or stored by TriviaGrid.

7. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights regarding your personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Because we do not sell personal information and collect only limited non-personally-identifiable data through standard web analytics and advertising, most CCPA provisions concerning the sale of data do not apply to us.

California residents may request information about any personal data we have collected about them by contacting us at the address below. We will respond to verifiable requests within 45 days.

8. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites. These linked sites are not under our control and we are not responsible for the privacy practices of any third-party sites. We encourage you to be aware when you leave our Site and to read the privacy statements of each website that collects personal information.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information on our servers from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
